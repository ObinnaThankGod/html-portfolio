Our Story

Rainbow Brush Face Painting began as a weekend hobby at a friend's birthday party and grew into a full-time crew of artists who live for the moment a kid sees their new face in the mirror for the first time. Every artist on our team is trained in hygiene best practices and uses only professional, skin-safe paints so parents can relax and enjoy the party too.
